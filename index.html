Privacy Policy

Wet Wet Games ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect personal data through our games. If you contact us via the website form, we only receive the information you explicitly provide (name, email) to respond to your inquiry.

2. Data Usage

Your contact information is used solely for communication between you and Wet Wet Games. We never sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for site functionality and performance monitoring. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.
